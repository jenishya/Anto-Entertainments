VIGNESH ANTONY (CEO/FOUNDER)

Vignesh Antony the founder and CEO of Anto entertainments and Media, a budding star in television based firm. Vignesh’s passion for innovation began at a young age and has been the driving force behind his successful career.

Prior to starting Anto Entertainments , he was a performer at KPY9 VIJAY TELEVISION, runner up in CK ZEE TAMIZH. He has also been a mimicry artist and have given a voice over in Tamil movies and cartoons. One of the notch's in his belt is that he did an exceptional voice over to late actor vivek in his last movie. He has also hosted shows and has also been invited as the guest of honor, key speaker and so on in maay institues colleges and events.
However, he became increasingly creativity and bureaucracy in these organizations and decided to start up on his own. With a small team of developers, he began building huge steps for his event production media. Over time, his vision and determination paid off, and Anto entertainments and Media has become a success.
Today, Vignesh continues to Encourage young minds like him to explore talents by providing opportunities in his firm. His leadership and vision have made a respected name in television and his influence can be felt throughout the industry. He also belives that mentoring young minds can not only ensure inspiration when the going gets tough, but they can also tap into experience.
